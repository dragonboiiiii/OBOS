Why OpenBeleeBOS?

OpenBeleeBOS is a modern x86_64 mobile OS that runs APKs and IPAs, while looking straight out of 2011. Exclusive to BeleePad, it’s open source, every commit ships with an .img, and it supports flashing Android images with a single tap.
